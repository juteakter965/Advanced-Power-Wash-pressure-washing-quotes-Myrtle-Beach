Important Tips for Requesting Pressure Washing Quotes

‚úì Best Practices for Quote Requests

Request written quotes from at least 3-5 companies for accurate comparison. Provide identical information to all companies to ensure comparable estimates. Ask for itemized breakdowns showing labor, materials, and additional fees. Request certificates of insurance with quotes. Clarify what's included and excluded from pricing. Get timeline estimates and completion dates in writing. Ask about guarantees and warranty terms. Request references from recent similar projects.

‚ö† Quote Red Flags to Avoid

Verbal-only quotes without written documentation. Vague pricing like "around $XXX" without specifics. Requires full payment upfront before work begins. Significantly lower than all other quotes (40%+ cheaper). No insurance documentation provided with quote. Pressure to sign immediately with "today only" discounts. Unwilling to provide references or detailed scope of work. Evasive answers about credentials or experience.

‚Ñπ Understanding Quote Timeframes

Most professional companies provide free quotes within 24-48 hours of request. In-person estimates typically schedule within 3-5 days. Complex projects may require 48-72 hours for detailed quotes. Rush quotes are possible but may cost extra. Written documentation should arrive within 24 hours after in-person assessment. Quotes typically remain valid for 30 days unless otherwise specified. Always confirm quote validity period before making decisions.
